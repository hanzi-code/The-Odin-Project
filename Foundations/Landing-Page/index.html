The 4 Seasons

The four seasons—spring, summer, autumn, and winter—bring distinct changes to the environment. Spring is a time of renewal, with flowers blooming and temperatures warming. Summer is hot, filled with long days and outdoor activities. Autumn brings cooler weather and colorful foliage, while winter is cold, often with snow and shorter days. Each season offers a unique experience, shaping the year in its own way.
